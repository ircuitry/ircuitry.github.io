Community workflows

Whole bots, ready to run: games, reminders, quizzes and more. Hit Copy, then paste the workflow straight into ircuitry. Built one? Open a PR.

Two ways to import:
  1. Install in app (one click): opens ircuitry and adds the workflow as a new bot tab. Needs ircuitry installed (Linux for now; the button is a no-op elsewhere).
  2. Copy, then in ircuitry click an empty spot on the canvas and press Ctrl+V. Or Download and use IMPORT (also accepts a dragged .ircbot). Works everywhere.
Then open the Inspector, fill in your server, nick and channels, and press RUN BOT. Workflows can include Code nodes that run on your machine, so review one before running it.
