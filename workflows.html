Community workflows

Whole bots, ready to run: games, reminders, quizzes and more. Hit Copy, then paste the workflow straight into ircuitry. Built one? Open a PR.

Import a copied workflow:
  1. In ircuitry, click an empty spot on the canvas and press Ctrl+V to drop the workflow's nodes in.
  2. Or use Download and click IMPORT in ircuitry to open it as a new bot tab (you can also drag the .ircbot onto the canvas).
Then open the Inspector, fill in your server, nick and channels, and press RUN BOT. Workflows can include Code nodes that run on your machine, so review one before running it.
