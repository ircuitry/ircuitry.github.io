Community themes

Reskin ircuitry from cream-and-pastel to a moody dark or a crisp high-contrast look. Hit Install to preview a theme live in the app, then keep it or revert. Made one in the Appearance editor? Open a PR.

Three ways to use a theme:
  1. Install in app (one click): opens ircuitry and applies the theme as a live preview - Keep it or revert. Needs ircuitry installed (Windows, macOS or Linux). On macOS, move ircuitry.app to Applications and open it once first.
  2. Copy, then in ircuitry open Appearance > Import / Export, paste it, press Preview, then Keep. Works everywhere.
  3. Download the .irctheme and drop it in ~/ircuitry/themes/, or load it from Appearance.
Prefer to roll your own? Open Appearance from the titlebar menu and tune every colour, font and feel knob from scratch.
